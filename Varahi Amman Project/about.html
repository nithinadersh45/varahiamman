About

Welcome to our revered Varahi Temple, a divine sanctuary where worship and spiritual practices flourish in devotion to Goddess Varahi. Join us for regular Poojas, Homams, and the sacred Bairavar Pali, all held with deep reverence on each Panjami, twice every month.

Our temple is a place of spiritual solace and divine connection, attracting devotees from near and far. We invite you to experience the peace and sanctity of our rituals, immersing yourself in the profound blessings of the Goddess.
